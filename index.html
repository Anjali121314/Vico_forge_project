Welcome To

VICO FORGE PRIVATE LIMITED

It was established in the year 1982 at Vapi and started commercial production of Closed Die Forging for TRACTOR and AUTO INDUSTRY.

Since then, VICO FORGE has grown many fold and have specialized in the manufacturing of critical forging components like SHIFTER FORKS, STEERING KNUCKLES, CONNECTING RODS, CAPS, etc.

Read More

VICO FORGE

At Vico Forge, we are in the business of transformation. Our skilled artisans use a belt hammer press machine, exerting a massive pressure of around 1500 tons, to shape our products. This process is more than just manufacturing - it’s an art form. Each piece we create is unique, crafted with precision and care. But this is only the beginning of the journey.

Read more

VICO MACHINERY

The semi-completed products from Vico Forge enter our second division - Vico Machinery. Here, they undergo further refinement using advanced machinery like CNC and polishing equipment. This is where our products truly come to life, as they are transformed into finished goods. At Vico Machinery, we have better control over the process and the quality of our products, ensuring that each item we produce meets our high standards of excellence.
