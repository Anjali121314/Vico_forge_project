  • Step 1

    Review machining drawings

    In Vico Forge, machining drawings are indispensable for guiding the fabrication of intricate components with precision and reliability. These drawings facilitate seamless communication between designers and manufacturers, ensuring seamless production processes and high-quality outputs.

  • Step 2

    Cnc Lathe & Vmc M/C

    In Vico Forge, CNC (Computer Numerical Control) lathes and VMC (Vertical Machining Center) machines are advanced tools used in manufacturing. CNC lathes specialize in rotating workpieces, while VMC machines perform various operations on stationary workpieces, both enhancing precision and efficiency in production processes.

  • Step 3

    Quality Control

    In Vico Forge, Quality control is vital in manufacturing, ensuring products meet specified standards. Through systematic inspection and testing, defects are identified and corrected, maintaining consistency and reliability in production, and ultimately satisfying customer expectations.

  • Step 4

    Complete Run

    A "Complete Run" in Vico Forge refers to the entire manufacturing process of a specific component or product, from start to finish. It encompasses all stages, including design, machining, quality control, and final inspection, ensuring the production meets requirements and standards.

    Process Order Per Specifications

    A "Process Order Per Specifications" in Vico Forge refers to executing manufacturing tasks precisely according to provided specifications. This entails adhering to detailed instructions regarding materials, dimensions, tolerances, and quality standards throughout the production process, ensuring the final product meets customer requirements accurately.
