Infrastructure

Our factory is situated on a 4670 sq. mtrs plot, with a built-up area of 2300 sq. mtrs, providing ample space for our operations. We’re powered by a 1200 KVA supply from the Gujarat Electricity Board, ensuring smooth operation of our machinery. Additionally, we have a 320 KVA backup generator, safeguarding our operations against any power disruptions.

LABORATORY:

Our establishment is comprehensively outfitted with advanced facilities that enable us to conduct meticulous inspections on incoming materials and components. These inspections encompass a broad spectrum of tests, including metallurgical, chemical, and physical analyses. This rigorous process ensures the quality and integrity of the materials and components we receive, thereby maintaining the high standards of our operations.

RAW MATERIAL STORAGE YARD:

Within our facility, we have a well-organized storage system specifically designed to accommodate raw materials of various grades and sections. Each material is meticulously labeled with distinct identification marks, ensuring quick and accurate recognition. This methodical approach not only facilitates efficient retrieval and usage of materials but also minimizes the risk of errors in the production process.

DIE –SHOP:

Our facility is fully equipped to fabricate dies and tooling, even those with complex shapes and specifications. We’ve recently enhanced our capabilities by acquiring a Vertical Machining Centre machine from Makino-Japan. This state-of-the-art equipment accelerates the die fabrication process while ensuring precision and accuracy, thereby streamlining our operations and maintaining the high quality of our products.

DESIGN OFFICE:

Our team is proficient in using Computer-Aided Design (CAD) software to design tooling and prepare detailed drawings of components. This technology-driven approach allows us to create precise and complex designs with ease. It also facilitates modifications and iterations, ensuring that the final design meets the exact specifications. Moreover, CAD enables us to visualize the components and tooling in a three-dimensional space.

RAW MATERIAL CUTTING SHOP:

At our unit, our Hacksaws, Bandsaws, and Shearing Presses are equipped to efficiently handle materials up to 100 mm RCS, ensuring precision and reliability in cutting processes. This capability enables us to meet diverse cutting needs with accuracy and effectiveness, enhancing overall operational efficiency. Our machinery ensures precise cutting of materials up to 100 mm RCS.

FORGE SHOP:

Various hammers ranging from 0.75 to 3.00 tons, along with a 1600-ton Russian forging press, are installed and utilized for producing forging components. These machinery assets facilitate efficient production processes, ensuring the creation of high-quality forging components to meet diverse industrial demands.

HEAT TREATMENT :

Oil fired pusher type continuous furnaces are used for normalizing, Polymer and Water quenching bath for hardening and Oil fired air circulating fan, pusher type furnace for tempering being used. Facilities for checking BHN hardness and micro structure are also available.

SHOT BLASTING:

Currently, the company possesses two shot blasting machines, comprising one twin table and the other horizontal drum type. These machines contribute to the efficient surface preparation of components, enhancing their quality and ensuring they meet the required standards for various industrial applications.

FINISH PRODUCTION:

A variety of grinding equipment, including bench grinders, belt grinders, disc grinders, and flexible grinders, are readily available for grinding forged components. This range of grinders ensures versatility and efficiency in achieving precise finishing and meeting quality standards for the forged products.

MAGNAFLUX INSPECTION:

Two units of magnetic crack detectors are employed to detect surface cracks in forging components. These devices play a crucial role in ensuring the integrity and quality of the forged products by accurately identifying any surface imperfections or defects that may compromise their structural integrity.

INSPECTION & QUALITY ASSURANCE:

Stage inspection is being carried out at every stage of production of dies, tooling, forging production and processing. Process sheets and check sheets are also provided at various stages to achieve proper quality of products. Sampling inspection and lot inspection is also carried out to assure quality.

STORES:

Ample open and covered area is provided to store the forged components and stores materials. Scrap open yard and die yard also available. Movement of material from Raw Material to forged components; green forging, heat-treated and finished goods are arranged in such a way that mixing up cannot take place in any stage. Bins are provided for cut pieces and forged components for proper identification.

RECORDS:

At every stage of production from receipt of raw material to finished products. Dies preparations to die conditioning after run of production are being recorded. Heat codes are also provided to material of different heats, colour identification also done to separate the lots to avoid mixing up.

DISPATCH:

Before dispatch, Dimensional Inspection and Metallurgical Inspection reports are meticulously prepared for each part. Quality Assurance then certifies the dispatched materials after ensuring the quality of components, guaranteeing they meet the required standards and specifications, thus ensuring customer satisfaction and compliance with quality assurance protocols..
