VICO FORGE PRIVATE LIMITED

It is a renowned manufacturing company located in Vapi, Gujarat, India. Since our establishment in 1982, we have been at the forefront of the Closed Die Forging industry, serving the Tractor and Auto Industry with unparalleled dedication and quality.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality, reliable, and efficient forging components that drive the success of our clients in the tractor and auto industry.

Our Facilities

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities are equipped with

1600 Ton Forging Press

This heavy-duty press is used for shaping metal using compressive force. The high capacity ensures it can handle large-scale and high-volume production.

Hammers ranging from 0.75 Ton to 3.0 Ton

These hammers are used in the forging process to shape the metal pieces. The range of weights allows for flexibility in manufacturing different products.

Modern Die-Shop

This is where the dies used in the forging process are made and maintained. A modern die-shop ensures precision and quality in the products.

State-of-art Heat Treatment plant

Heat treatment is a critical process in metalworking. It involves heating and cooling to achieve desired properties like increased hardness, temperature resistance, ductility, and strength.

Well-equipped finish production

This refers to the final stages of production where the forged parts are finished and prepared for delivery. This could involve processes like machining, coating, and inspection for quality control.

Specially manufactured Shot Peening Machine for Connecting Rods

Shot peening is a process used to improve the properties of metal parts. In this case, a specially manufactured machine is used for shot peening connecting rods, enhancing their fatigue resistance and longevity.
